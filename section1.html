Geographic information systems can be widely used in veterinary monitoring, recording, and reporting disease information. People can use geographic information systems to make some complex maps, such as disease incidence, mortality, and so on. In the event of an infectious disease, it is efficient and accurate to use geographic information systems to identify outbreak locations and at-risk areas. Through mapping, farms in danger can be notified and make the right response in a short time.
