In a broad sense, remote sensing refers to the activities of recording, observing and perceiving objects or events in distant places. This is a technology for obtaining information without physical contact with an object (Bhabhor and Vyas, 2018). In the field of agriculture and animal husbandry, remote sensing technology plays an important role. Remote sensing technology provides a variety of ways to implement the survey of agriculture and animal husbandry. The application of remote sensing allows people to quickly obtain a large amount of information about a target, such as crop production, crop yield, livestock numbers, animal sex and animal distribution.
