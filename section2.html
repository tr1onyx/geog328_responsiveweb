This is a project in China, called Qinghai Livestock Network. The animal networking product is a supplementary design based on the monitoring means required by the loan live animal risk control platform of Qinghai Unicom and Agricultural Bank of China. The core demand side is the live animal loan project Department of Agricultural Bank of China, and the core demand is to regularly supervise the live animals pledged by loans to reduce the loss of assets and loan risks.
